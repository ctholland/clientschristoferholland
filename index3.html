Get Started Learn My Process

Tour Agreement

To tour properties, you'll sign a Tour Agreement—a short, non-exclusive agreement now required by NY State. It simply confirms I'm your agent for any listings I show you.

Buyer Representation

When you're serious about purchasing, we'll move into a formal Buyer Representation Agreement. This exclusive contract ensures I can fully advocate for you and negotiate on your behalf.

My Approach

  • 100% Commission Structure: My time and energy go toward serious buyers ready to engage in the process. I'm selective for your benefit as much as mine.
  • Take Your Time: If you're still browsing or researching, I encourage it. That's an important part of the process—I'm here when you're ready.
  • Always Available: I'm always one call, text, or email away for questions or guidance throughout your journey.

Steps to Work Together

1

Initial Inquiry

Reach out with a property, budget, or timeline in mind. We'll discuss your needs, preferences, and what you're looking for in your next home.

2

Tour Agreement

For early showings or previews, you'll sign a basic Tour Agreement (required by NYS). This protects both of us and establishes our working relationship.

3

Buyer Representation Agreement

When ready to move seriously, we'll formalize the relationship. This ensures I can prioritize your search and fully advocate for your interests.

4

Full Search Strategy

I'll build a personalized search plan based on your criteria and move quickly on listings. We'll tour properties strategically and efficiently.

5

Offer to Close

I'll guide you through the offer process, negotiations, board approval (if applicable), and final closing. Full support every step of the way.
