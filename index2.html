Working With Me

Welcome—I’m genuinely excited to support you in finding a home that fits the next chapter of your life. Here’s how I work, so you know what to expect from the start.

Tours: To tour properties, you'll sign a Tour Agreement, which is a short, non-exclusive agreement now required by NY State. It simply confirms I’m your agent for any listings I show you.

Buyer Representation: When you’re serious about purchasing, we’ll move into a formal Buyer Representation Agreement. This exclusive contract ensures I can fully advocate for you, prioritize your search, and negotiate on your behalf.

I work on 100% commission—meaning my time and energy go toward serious buyers ready to engage in the process. I’m selective for your benefit as much as mine.

If you’re still browsing or researching, I encourage it. That’s an important part of the process. Feel free to take your time—I’m here when you’re ready. I’m always one call, text, or email away.

Steps to Work Together

  1. Initial Inquiry: Reach out with a property, budget, or timeline in mind.
  2. Tour Agreement: For early showings or previews, you'll sign a basic Tour Agreement (required by NYS).
  3. Buyer Representation Agreement: When ready to move seriously, we’ll formalize the relationship.
  4. Full Search Strategy: I’ll build a personalized search plan and move quickly on listings.
  5. Offer to Close: I’ll guide you through the offer process, negotiations, board approval, and final closing.

I look forward to helping you navigate this next chapter with clarity, confidence, and care.
